About

Josh Milburn is a professional engineer working on energy access problems with Angaza Design. He enjoys the technical challenges of firmware development and mixed-signal design, and finds working with a distributed team to accomplish high-impact development goals very satisfying.

About the site

A place to put (mostly technical) discoveries that come up in the course of my work. Likely something of interest to those designing cost-efficient hardware for monitoring and control and M2M applications (or, if you're enamored of publicity and fancy names for old concepts, "IoT").
